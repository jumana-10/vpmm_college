VPMM Advantage

Arul Tharum VPMM College of Engineering and Technology is a leading engineering college for women located in Virudhunagar, Tamil Nadu, India. Established in 2002, the college is affiliated with Anna University Chennai and is approved by AICTE. The college offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various engineering disciplines, including Computer Science, Electronics and Communication Engineering, Electrical and Electronics Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, and Civil Engineering.
The college has a team of highly qualified and experienced faculty members who are dedicated to providing quality education to students. The college follows a practical approach to teaching, with weekly exams and regular assessments to prepare students for final exams. The college also has well-equipped laboratories, libraries, and other facilities to support the learning process.
